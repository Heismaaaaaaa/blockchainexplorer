Brand Guidelines & Assets

Thank you for your support in using Blockscan. Below, we have provided a few guidelines for using our brand assets.

01. Brand Name

"Blockscan" is one word and spelled with a capital B.

02. Brand Guidelines

You may use the Blockscan name and logo in your website or app as long as you adhere to the Blockscan branding guidelines.

  • Do not alter the Blockscan logo.
  • The logo is to be used as provided.
  • Do not combine Blockscan logo with other images without consent.
  • Do not use the Blockscan brand in any way that implies a relationship, affiliation, or endorsement. For example, "we partner with Blockscan."
  • Do reference Blockscan as follows: "Powered by Blockscan" "Data provided by Blockscan" "Source: Blockscan"
  • Do not use the Blockscan brand in conjunction with any illegal and unlawful activity, promotion, and product.
